Actress from 'Scream' is fired Sequel for Pro-Palestine Posts

Actress Melissa Barrera has been dismissed from the "Scream" franchise due to her social media posts related to the Israel-Hamas conflict. Known for her role as Sam Carpenter in "Scream 6," Barrera will not be returning for the sequel, as her supportive posts for Palestine led to her removal from the cast of "Scream VII."

The Last of Us Second Season

The highly anticipated second season of The Last of Us, originally set for 2025, faces delays due to the writers' strike and other setbacks. Production is set to begin in early 2024, with the release now scheduled for the following year. Uncertainty about the series' future beyond the second season adds anticipation. Despite the extended wait, creators emphasize a commitment to quality, and discussions around the potential for a grand finale are underway.

Explosive Revelations: MAPPA Studio's Controversial Treatment of Jujutsu Kaisen Animators

The anime season highlights Jujutsu Kaisen, a leading shounen series with a dedicated fan base. However, discussions have arisen regarding how studio MAPPA handles its staff. In late September, it was disclosed that MAPPA mandated its employees to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), preventing them from revealing any information about working conditions during Gege Akutami's anime production. Despite MAPPA's attempt to maintain confidentiality, the leaked contract has emboldened Jujutsu Kaisen professionals to speak out against the studio, sharing their experiences on social media.
